Flower Embroidery

Materials

  • Thread
  • Threading needle
  • Pen

This was my first time looking into embroidery. I have seen and heard of it but it has always scared me because it looks complicated. However, I stumbled upon a tiktok of someone embroidering jeans and thought that maybe I could do it. I also had spare friendship thread and my roomate happened to have a needle so it was a sign.

I searched for a video and I used the one below!

Since this was my first time I decided to test out my skills on an old pair of skinny jeans that I knew I was probably not going to use. I followed along with the video and found that it was not as hard as I thought it was going to be. The person in the video did a great job at explaining everything.

I started by only doing a few flowers but day by day I would start adding more. Overall I love the finished product and can't wait to do another project.

This is a project I would recommend to anyone, cost of materials are cheap and it does not require a lot of school to begin.
